Max Moe

IT Support Specialist | WordPress Developer | Photographer

It all began in the era of Windows XP—a time when floppy disks were still in circulation, and the sound of a dial-up modem signaled the start of a day’s work. I wasn’t just fascinated by computers—I was determined to master them. From the moment I first sat down at a Windows 3.1 machine, I knew technology would shape my path.

Over the years, I’ve journeyed through the evolution of operating systems—from Windows 3.1 through 11, alongside macOS and legacy Macintosh systems. I’ve not just watched these systems change, I’ve lived them—working with everything from the earliest Pentium processors to the most advanced, modern IT infrastructures. These foundational experiences forged my deep understanding of hardware and software, laying the groundwork for my future career.

But this wasn’t just a technical journey—it was an adventure of problem-solving and innovation. As an IT Support Specialist, I’ve tackled challenges that tested my ability to diagnose, troubleshoot, and resolve complex technical issues with precision. Each new version of software or hardware brought its own set of puzzles, and I thrived in the process of solving them.

Yet, I didn't stop at hardware. Over a decade ago, I ventured into the world of WordPress Development, diving into the back-end complexities of themes, plugins, and post types. From North America to Europe and the Middle East, I’ve worked with businesses to create custom WordPress solutions—websites that aren’t just visually stunning but are optimized for performance, SEO, and user experience. I’ve witnessed high-traffic sites flourish under my care, growing faster, more efficient, and more impactful than ever before.

And just like how I craft WordPress sites, I also craft moments through my lens as a Photographer—capturing high-quality visuals that tell stories of their own.

For more than 15 years, I’ve blended my passion for technology with a creative spark—continuously evolving, learning, and mastering the art of tech support, development, and digital creativity. Whether it’s troubleshooting complex IT issues, developing powerful WordPress sites, or capturing stunning visuals with my camera, I'm always ready for the next challenge.

My name is Muhaisen, but I often go by Max or Moe. With a strong technical background and a passion for problem-solving, I bring creativity and efficiency to every project I undertake.
